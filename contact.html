Contact Us

Wholesome Horizons Learning Center has been in operation since August 10, 2015. Please contact me regarding any current openings, for more information about our center, and to schedule a tour of our facilities where you will also receive our new family packet containing our policies, procedures, rates, and other important information. Please provide information about your child or children including ages, and hours of care needed.

For school-age children, public school bus service is provided to and from Elizabeth Eichelberger Elementary School.  

We are also looking for qualified individuals to serve as assistants and/or substitutes. Prior experience with young children is a must and a teaching background is preferred. Please contact us for more information on applying for a position.
