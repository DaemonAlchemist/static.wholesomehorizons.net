Wholesome Horizons Learning Center: A Home Child Care in Bolingbrook, IL

Wholesome Horizons Learning Center is a licensed home child care and preschool located in Bolingbrook, IL, convenient to the Stevenson Expressway (I-55). Wholesome Horizons provides a structured, nurturing environment at an affordable cost. Infants and toddlers are provided an environment where they can explore through play and develop physically, socially, emotionally, and cognitively. Preschoolers work towards gaining the social, emotional, and academic skills necessary for them to be successful in kindergarten and beyond. School age children receive a structured before and after school program that allows them time to complete homework, extend their learning, and/or relax after a long day! We place a SERIOUS emphasis on play at all ages! ☺ Visit our curriculum page for more information.
